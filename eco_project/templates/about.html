About EcoPet!

Welcome to EcoPet! We want to help you explore the University of Exeter campus while keeping your pet happy and healthy.

EcoPet is a platform designed to help pet owners discover new locations, challenges, and connect with your community. You can scan QR codes at sustainable locations around the campus to score points for your pet so you can keep them happy and earn them points!

Points are spendable in the item shop so you can show off your pet to your friends and other users.
